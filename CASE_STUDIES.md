# Public AI Case Studies

## Enterprise Knowledge Assistant

Problem:
Organizations struggle to access internal knowledge distributed across documents, procedures and policies.

Solution:
RAG architecture with retrieval, grounding and controlled answer generation.

Capabilities:
- knowledge retrieval
- onboarding acceleration
- internal support
- policy lookup

## Executive KPI Copilot

Problem:
Leadership teams spend significant time consolidating operational information.

Solution:
AI-assisted KPI summarization, anomaly detection and decision support.

Capabilities:
- KPI summaries
- weekly reporting
- executive dashboards
- operational alerts

## Agentic Operations Platform

Problem:
Operational teams perform repetitive coordination activities across systems.

Solution:
Tool-calling agents with approval workflows and execution boundaries.

Capabilities:
- workflow automation
- approvals
- task routing
- operational recommendations

## Enterprise AI Governance

Problem:
Organizations need safe AI adoption.

Solution:
Governance framework including RBAC, auditability, review layers and monitoring.

Capabilities:
- audit trails
- approval workflows
- risk controls
- observability
